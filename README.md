# Meridian
> Guiding you through the research landscape

## What is Meridian?
Meridian is a full-stack research discovery platform that aggregates 
NIH and NSF grant data with academic publications, allowing researchers, 
research administrators, and students to search, explore, and discover 
funding opportunities and research activity in their domain.

## The Problem
University researchers and administrators have no unified place to 
explore the funding landscape. Grant data, publication data, and 
researcher networks live in separate silos — NIH Reporter, OpenAlex, 
institutional systems — with no way to see how they connect. Meridian 
solves this.

## Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** React + TypeScript
- **Backend:** Python + FastAPI
- **Database:** PostgreSQL
- **Graph Database:** Neo4j
- **Vector Search:** pgvector
- **Visualization:** D3.js
- **Deployment:** Vercel (frontend), Render (backend), Neon (database)
